hackathon

(n.) an event in which computer programmers and others in the field of software development, like graphic designers, interface designers, project managers and computational philologists, collaborate intensively on software projects.

Our first ever hackathon will be taking place on November the 17th in the Library (3rd Floor) of the Mathematical and Computer Science Building at Kent State University. We have a wide range of sponsors helping us put on this event. This hackathon is open to students from any University. Feel free to email hacKSU@cs.kent.edu with any questions you may have.


Sign Up

To sign up first register at Hacker League and then join our Hackathon page. Then submit any project ideas that you may have to do at the hackathon. Don't forget to assemble your team!
