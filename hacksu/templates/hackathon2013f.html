Kent Hack Enough 2013

Ohio's largest hackathon is back and is ready to make an ever bigger bang! This Fall Semester Hacksu will be hosting their annual hackathon from October 18th to the 20th. We have a surprise in store for everyone. No longer will hackers be stuck in the narrow and crammed halls of the Math Building. This year the hackathon will be taking place in the Kent State University Library!
