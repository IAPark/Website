Who are we?

hacksu is a student organization at Kent State University focused on learning and utilizing contemporary web and mobile technologies. We are a very diverse community, spanning multiple majors and are open to students of any skill level. Everyone has something to learn!

What do we teach?

We teach a wide variety of technologies such as jQuery Mobile, Python, Django, Ruby, Rails, Node.js, Android, iOS, Heroku, Google App Engine and much more. All of this is in hopes that you fall in love with one or many of these technologies and construct something cool.

How do I get involved?

We recommend coming to our weekly meetings, held during the semester, in the Math and Computer Science building at 7:00pm. You can also join our Facebook page and follow us on Twitter to see what we are up to!
