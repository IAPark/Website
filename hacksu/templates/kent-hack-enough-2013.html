Kent Hack Enough  What


Kent Hack Enough, Hacksu's annual hackathon, brings the passion and innovation behind hackathons to the Midwest. With over 150 participants, Kent Hack Enough will be the largest hackathon in Ohio. Top prizes will include cash and exposure to top companies. Admission is completely free for students, and travel costs will be reimbursed.

Date & Time  When

Kent Hack Enough will be held on October 18th starting at 6:00pm and run until 2:00pm on October 20th.
